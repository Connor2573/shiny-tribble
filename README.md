# PowerUp2018

Final Button Mappings
Left Joy: Cartesian Movement
	1(Trigger) - Toggle
	2 - Arm Down
	3 - Arm Up
	4 - Strafe Left
	5 - Strafe Right
	6 - Open Claw
	7 - Close Claw
Right Joy: 
X-axis= rotation
	6-11 - “Robot Shaper Functions” lowest to highest of the pinches
	6=lowest
	7=close cube
	8=exchange
	9=switch
	10=transit high
	11=scale high
	3 - Magic button(full)
Third Joy
	8 - go to final position
	2 - Drive Back
	3 - Drive Forward
	4 - Drive Left
	5 - Drive Right
	6 - Arm Up
	7 - Arm Down
	10 - Aim, partial magic button
	11 - Magic button(full)
